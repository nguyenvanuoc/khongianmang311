Deputy Minister Le Quan receives Mr. Hans Diete Sauer, President of F&U Group

On November 2nd, 2017, at the Head office of Ministry of Labour - Invalids and Social Affairs, Deputy Minister Le Quan had a cordial meeting and working with Mr. Hans Diete Sauer, President of F&U Group - the Organization on Education and Training in Germany working in the fields of language and many international occupations. Attending the meeting were leadership representatives from the Department of International Cooperation, the Directorate of Vocational Education and representatives from F&U Office in Vietnam.
